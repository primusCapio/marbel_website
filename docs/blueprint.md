# **App Name**: Stone Craft Hub

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Displays a clean, premium hero section with high-quality stone images, business tagline, and direct call-to-action to contact via WhatsApp.
- Categorized Product Catalog: Browse a comprehensive catalog of marble, granite, and Kota stone products, organized by category, with images, names, and brief descriptions.
- Product Detail Pages: View individual product details including large image galleries, comprehensive descriptions, and an inquiry button (WhatsApp/Contact Form).
- Business Information (About Us): A dedicated page to convey the company's introduction, experience, and commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
- Integrated Contact & Inquiry System: A contact page with phone number, Google Maps location, and a direct contact form, along with omnipresent WhatsApp buttons for immediate inquiries.
- AI Stone Advisor: An AI tool that suggests optimal stone types based on user inputs such as project type, desired aesthetic, and functional requirements.
- Responsive & Performant Design: Ensures the website is fully mobile-friendly, adapting seamlessly to all screen sizes, and is optimized for fast loading and smooth navigation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, dark desaturated blue (#3D4D5C) to evoke professionalism and depth, ideal for headlines and primary interactive elements.
- Background color: An ethereal, very light blue-gray (#F0F2F4), providing a clean canvas that complements the primary color and highlights natural stone textures.
- Accent color: A clean, vibrant aqua-cyan (#33CCCC), strategically used for call-to-action buttons, active states, and key highlights to draw attention and add a touch of modern elegance.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and objective readability, aligning with a premium and professional aesthetic suitable for all textual content.
- Use minimalist, clean line icons that complement the modern UI, particularly for navigation, contact information, and product features. Icons should enhance usability without distracting from the stone imagery.
- Employ a generous use of whitespace and a modular grid system, especially for product displays, to create an uncluttered, high-end visual experience. Implement a sticky navbar and a floating WhatsApp button for consistent access.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions for page changes and elegant hover effects on interactive elements like product cards and buttons, enhancing the premium feel without being ostentatious.
